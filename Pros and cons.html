Smart Landmine Detection Rover

An innovative project combining technology and humanitarian efforts.

Pros

  • Efficient landmine detection, reducing the risk of accidents.
  • Remote-controlled operation allows for safe exploration of hazardous areas.
  • Integration of Arduino and NodeMCU enables real-time data transmission.
  • Potential to save lives and aid in humanitarian efforts.

Cons

  • Requires initial setup and calibration.
  • Relies on stable internet connectivity for remote operation.
  • May encounter obstacles or limitations in challenging terrain.
  • Cost of components and maintenance may be prohibitive in some contexts.
